S&M 2

Metallica are quite conscious of their story, always making sure they do something unique to celebrate milestone anniversaries for their past triumphs. Last night (September 6) in San Francisco, the group saluted the 20th anniversary of their S&M orchestral live album wiht the first of two S&M 2 concerts.

Performing on a revolving circular stage of the San Francisco Symphony and conductor Edwin Outwater. It was the symphony who led the charge, performing "The Ecstasy of Gold", which has hitorically served as the opening salvo for Metallica's concerts before they hit the stage.
